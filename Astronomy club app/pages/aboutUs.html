About Us


Our Mission

We created RSS to provide students with an extracirrcular about a topic that wasn't commonly addressed in our school: Astronomy. Whether you feel your sole purpose in life is to study the wonders of space or you just like looking at the cool objects that exist in our vast universe, RSS is a club where you can further your cosmic interests alongside people who are there to do the same.

What you can do in RSS:

Research


Conduct formal research and have your articles published on this website for everyone else to see - don't worry, we'll help you perfect your articles along the way!

Competitions


Participate in various local competitions throughout the school year, competing in topics related to various STEM fields.

Science Fair


Show off your talents in a schoolwide science fair we hope to host later in the school year. Stay tuned for more information!

For more information about competitions and the science fair, visit the events page.

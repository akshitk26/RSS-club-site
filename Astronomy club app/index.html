What is RSS?

It's more than just a club.

RSS is a place to unravel the secrets of the universe alongside likeminded peers. Research space topics, participate in competitions, and show off your skills in school-wide events.

Should I join RSS?

This is a place for more than just space enthusiasts. Like technology? Dive into the latest cutting edge research being done on advanced space systems to support space colonization. Like biology? Explore the different applications of botany for permanent extraterrestrial landscapes. No matter your interests - theres a space for you here.

Plus, we, the creators of this club, aren't space freaks either! We just find astronomy interesting and hope to help others find there interests too.
